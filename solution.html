Deutsches Museum , Munich

The Deutsches Museum is ont of the premier museum of science and technology in all of Europe. Here you will find section devoted to manufacturing, ships, energy production, and aviation.

There's a planetarium on the top floor; an outdoor area gives you a fantastic view of the city. You might not expect to see exhibits of musical instruments and technical toys, but this museum has them. For people who like hands-on exhibits, there's an entire area where you can do simple physics experiments.The museum even has a working DNA lab!

The museum is open from 9 a.m to 5 p.m every day except some holidays.The S-Bahn Isartot and U-Bahn Fraunhoferstabe stations are near the museum.
